Lulu Chicken Tenders by LuluChick!

Description

A very tender, chicken tenders! with the same taste as Lulu Chicken, but more tender and no bone!!!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tender
  • Flour
  • Secret Spices
  • Frying Oil
  • Love! Most Definitely!

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken tenders
  2. Heat the pan with oil
  3. Coat the chicken tenders with secret spices
  4. Deep fry the chicken tender!
  5. Serve hot!!!
